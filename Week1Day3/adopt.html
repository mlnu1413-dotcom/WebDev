Adoption process

A simple checklist that many shelters use. Always follow the rules of your local shelter.

  1. Browse adoptable pets and pick a good match for your lifestyle.
  2. Meet the pet in person (and bring family members if required).
  3. Complete an application and provide ID and proof of address.
  4. Pay adoption fee (often includes vaccines/spay-neuter depending on the shelter).
  5. Take your new pet home and schedule a follow-up vet visit.
